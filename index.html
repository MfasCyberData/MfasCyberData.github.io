About

I build reliable, secure, and traceable data pipelines that turn raw data into information teams can trust. My work centers on ingestion, transformation, database design, data quality, automation, and secure access management.

What sets my profile apart is the cybersecurity background. I do not treat security as an afterthought: I design with least-privilege access, auditability, and GDPR awareness from the start. My MSc covered both sides, from data engineering and data science to SOC operations, digital forensics, and EBIOS RM risk analysis, so I approach every data project with both an engineering mindset and a security-first perspective.
